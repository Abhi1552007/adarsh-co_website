Indirect Taxation (GST)

Goods and Services Tax (GST) compliance requires accurate reporting, timely filings and continuous reconciliation with statutory records. Adarsh & Co. provides end-to-end GST services for businesses across sectors.

Comprehensive GST solutions focused on compliance, accuracy and risk mitigation.

Our services cover registration, return filing, audits, assessments and refund support under the GST law.

GST Registration

GST registration is mandatory for eligible businesses based on turnover, nature of supply and place of operations.

Smooth and timely GST registration with correct classification.

We assist clients with registration for regular, composition, casual and non-resident taxable persons.

Our support ensures accurate documentation, faster approvals and compliance with statutory requirements.

GST Return Filing

GST return filing involves periodic submission of outward supplies, inward supplies, tax liability and payments.

Accurate and timely filing of all applicable GST returns.

We handle GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-4, GSTR-6 and other applicable returns based on business requirements.

Our process minimises errors, late fees and interest exposure.

Call Us: +91 98451 84260

GST Audit & Assessment

GST audits and assessments involve scrutiny of returns, books of accounts and tax payments by authorities.

Early identification of discrepancies and compliance gaps.

We assist with audit preparation, reconciliation and representation during departmental proceedings.

Our support helps reduce litigation risk and ensures smooth resolution.

Call Us: +91 98451 84260

GST Refunds

GST refunds arise from excess tax payments, exports, inverted duty structures and other eligible scenarios.

Structured refund applications with complete documentation.

We assist with filing, tracking and follow-up of GST refund claims to ensure timely processing.

Our approach reduces rejection risks and improves cash flow efficiency.

Call Us: +91 98451 84260

GSTR-9 & 9C

GSTR-9 and GSTR-9C involve annual reconciliation of GST returns with audited financial statements.

Accurate reconciliation to avoid notices and penalties.

We assist with preparation, reconciliation and certification of GSTR-9 and 9C as applicable.

Our service ensures statutory compliance and readiness for scrutiny.
