Audit & Assurance Services

Delivering independent, reliable and compliant Audit services that enhance transparency, strengthen governance and build stakeholder confidence.

Statutory Audit

Statutory Audits ensure compliance with the Companies Act and applicable regulatory requirements. Our approach focuses on accuracy, transparency and professional independence.

We follow a risk-based Audit methodology designed to identify material misstatements and strengthen internal controls.

Our Audits provide stakeholders with confidence in financial reporting while ensuring statutory compliance.

Tax Audit (Section 44AB)

Tax Audits under Section 44AB are mandatory for eligible businesses and professionals. We conduct a detailed examination of books of accounts in line with ICAI standards.

Includes preparation and filing of Form 3CD with Form 3CA / 3CB ensuring accuracy and timely compliance.

Our process helps reduce scrutiny risk and ensures smooth assessments.

GST Audit

GST Audits involve reconciliation of GST returns, books of accounts and tax payments. We help identify mismatches and exposure areas early.

Improves readiness for departmental scrutiny and minimizes penalties.

Our Audits ensure end-to-end GST compliance and documentation accuracy.
